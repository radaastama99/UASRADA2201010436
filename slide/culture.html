List of Beach Beauty in Kelingking Beach

The following are some of the beauties that we can enjoy at Kelingking Beach

Cliffs similar to T-Rex

The view of Kelingking Beach from above is an exotic view of Paluang Cliff, or what is often called Karang Dawa. Paluang is the name of the temple located not far from this cliff, while Karang Dawa is the name of the village. This cliff has a very unique shape. When viewed from above, it looks like the shape of a whale cub lying on the edge of the ocean, besides that, many also think it looks like the neck of a Tyrannosaurus Rex (T-Rex).

The terrain is quite extreme

The beach has land jutting into the sea like a little finger. To be able to enjoy Kelingking Beach, tourists must go down a steep cliff with a slope of about 70-80 degrees and only hold on to a makeshift wooden fence. Or you can use a ship that can be passed from the port to the coast.

Diving with manta rays

In addition to enjoying the beautiful hill scenery, come to Kelingking Beach, visitors can try diving at Manta Point. Unlike other diving places that only offer the beauty of unspoiled coral reefs with a variety of types of marine animals in them. When we dive at Manta Point, we can swim freely to see up close, and can even play together with dozens of manta rays. This location is no less crowded with divers like in Crystal Bay, so you have to be good at adjusting the position to get good visibility without disturbing other tourists.

Recognized by the World

Kelingking Beach its name is quite famous in the past two years. No wonder that crowded visitors come to this beach, especially when the holidays come. This beach is ranked as the 9th most beautiful beach in Asia according to CNN Travel. Travel review site Tripadvisor ranks Kelingking Beach as the second best in Asia and 19th for the best beaches in the world.
